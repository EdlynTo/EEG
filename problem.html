Present Day Elevators


The majority of current elevators require more energy going down than up. This is a big problem as there are over 2 billion buildings in the world. All of them require energy to move up and down floors. We can use gravity as a force to help move the EEG down without requiring massive amounts of energy. There is a lot of energy wasted that we could save with the help of EEG.
